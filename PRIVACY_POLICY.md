## 1. Who can use Spur
Spur is for people **18 years of age or older**. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we learn we have, we will delete it.

## 2. Information we collect

**You give us:**
- **Phone number** — for account creation and verification (one-time passcode).
- **Profile details** — your first name, an optional bio, a profile photo, and your activity interests.
- **Approximate location** — see Section 4. We collect a location you provide/permit; we **blur it to roughly 1 km before storing it** and never store your exact GPS position.
- **Payment information** — when an event requires a deposit, a card is collected. **Card numbers are handled entirely by Stripe; we never see or store your full card number.** We store a Stripe customer reference and a card fingerprint used only for fraud/abuse prevention.
- **Identity verification** — if you use ID verification, it is processed by Stripe Identity; we receive only a pass/fail result and verification level, not your ID document.
- **Content you create** — events, posts, messages, reviews, reports, and feedback.

**We collect automatically:**
- **Usage and device data** — app interactions, device type, and a push-notification token (if you enable notifications).
- **Check-in location** — when you check in to an event, your device reports a location used only to confirm you're near the venue.

## 3. How we use your information
- To create and run your account and the core features (events, RSVPs, host approvals, "It's ON" notifications).
- To verify your phone and, where applicable, your identity, and to keep the community genuine (fraud/abuse prevention, including preventing banned users from returning).
- To process deposits and no-show charges for events that have them.
- To show you relevant nearby plans and venues.
- To send you service notifications you've enabled.
- To keep people safe (reviewing reports, enforcing our rules, blocking/removing users).
- To comply with law.

## 4. Location — how we protect it
Your exact location is **never stored or shown to other users.** Coordinates are rounded/blurred to approximately 1 km on our servers before saving, so others only ever see your rough area. Check-in uses your device's reported location solely to confirm proximity to the venue at that moment.

## 5. How we share information
We do **not** sell your personal information. We share it only with:
- **Service providers ("processors")** who run parts of Spur for us, under contract: **Supabase** (database, authentication, storage), **Stripe** (payments and identity), **Twilio** (SMS one-time passcodes) [remove if not used], **Expo** (push notifications), and **Railway** (server hosting).
- **Other users**, only what your profile and posts are meant to show (e.g., first name, photo, reliability score, your public events) — never your phone number, exact location, or payment details.
- **Legal/safety** — if required by law, or to protect the safety of people or the platform.
- **Business transfers** — if Spur is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, information may transfer as part of that deal.

## 6. Data retention and account deletion
You can delete your account in-app at any time. When you do, we remove your profile and personal content. **For safety reasons, we retain a limited, irreversible "tombstone" record** — a one-way hash of your phone number and card fingerprint — so that users who were banned for serious violations cannot simply create a new account. This tombstone does not identify you to others and is not used for marketing.
